About School

Darbari Lal Foundation World School, strives to give every student the balanced learning that he dreamt of, by blending Indian cultural values with the finest in modern curriculum. The school endeavors to better itself with each step to reach excellence by looking inwards to excel outwards.

Darbari Lal Foundation is a trust, founded in the memory of distinguished educationist and visionary Shri Darbari Lal Ji. The Foundation is committed to setting up institutions like sports academies, libraries, centers for teacher training, educational research and international schools in greater noida.

DLWS is the Foundation's second school in crossings republik & greater noida region. DLWS Awarded Asia's Top 100 fastest growing Schools by the KPMG Survey (WCRC) in 2015 and No. 1 among all the best schools in crossings republik and greater noida.
